Who are The Train Rush?

Your hosts, Craig Taylor and Dave Moss, are the co-presenters of The Train Rush, a short form podcast, typically running 30-45 minutes in length, where they discuss a single 'train game' they have played in the preceding fortnight.

Focusing on 18xx games but also covers related games, when sufficiently compelling.
