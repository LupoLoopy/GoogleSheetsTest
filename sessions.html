Overall Event Timings

When do we start and finish, guv?

Saturday - 09:00 to 23:00
Sunday - 09:00 to 19:00

The venue opens at 9am each day. Proceedings will close at 11pm on Saturday and 7pm on Sunday, affording you time to travel home.

Planned games will be occuring during the windows listed below. Players planning on engaging in pick-up games should be aware of these timings as it will influence the availability of players during the event.

  • Saturday Day - 09:00 til 18:00
  • Saturday Evening- 18:00 til 23:00
  • Sunday Morning- 09:00 til 13:00
  • Sunday Afternoon- 13:00 til 19:00

It is suggested longer titles are scheduled during the Saturday day window, as we have a greater tolerance for overrun during said day.

Clarifications & Considerations

The small print...

  • Players are only eligible to host or signup for an event if they have already purchased a ticket for StationedOut.
  • Players will not be able to host and join a game scheduled at the same time. The event hosting will be deemed to take preference in this scenario.
  • The timings of these sessions are approximate; however the intention is to commence session 1 & 3 at the scheduled times. Sessions 2 & 4 may be impacted by long running games from the previous session.
  • Should the event a host / players sign up for not meet the minimum player requirement, then the host and players will be free to join other already scheduled games until they meet their capacity, with agreement of the game host.

Who are The Train Rush?

Your hosts, Craig Taylor and Dave Moss, are the co-presenters of The Train Rush, a short form podcast, typically running 30-45 minutes in length, where they discuss a single 'train game' they have played in the preceding fortnight.

Focusing on 18xx games but also covers related games, when sufficiently compelling.
