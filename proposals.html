StationedOut 2019

 brought to you by The Train Rush podcast

Periodically updated list of proposed pre-scheduled games for StationedOut 2019 train game convention, situated in Basingstoke, Hampshire.

Proposals for scheduled games

Working Preview - Subject to change

1822MRS - 1822 Medium Regional Scenario

18xx - Intermediate - Saturday 09:00-18:00

Host: Jon C

Ideal player count: 4
Remaining player spots: 3
18xx title using Euro-style mechanisms for auctioning off the small, medium and major companies which make up a player's portfolio. Seeking players who know how to play 18xx already, but not necessarily expert in this title.

1828 - 1830 writ large. Larger... Larger still... Got it.

18xx - Advanced - Saturday 09:00-18:00

Host: Craig Taylor

Ideal player count: 4
Remaining player spots: 3
Think 1830 with mergers, systems and LOTS of companies. A title designed by one of the hobbies most enthusastic analysts. Craig is fit to teach, but this title is intrinsically deep (if not aneurism inducingly complex), so longhorns requested. It would be helpful if folks could read the freely avaiable manual prior to the day too.

1817 - Short Stocks and Tall Orders

18xx - Advanced - Saturday 09:00-18:00

Host: Dave Moss

Ideal player count: 6
Remaining player spots: 5
Advanced title conceptually, if you haven't played it before. 'Mindblowing' on first play. One for long-horns or enthusastic and established students of the form.

1846 - The Race for the Midwest

18xx - Beginner - Saturday 18:00-23:00

Host: Sven K

Ideal player count: 4
Remaining player spots: 3
An introduction game for new players of 18xx using a respected teaching title.

18NewEngland [AAG Prototype] - The Growing Power of American Railroads

18xx - Intermediate - Saturday 18:00-23:00

Host: Dave Moss

Ideal player count: 5
Remaining player spots: 3
Take this opportunity to explore one of a few copies of 18NewEngland in the UK, under the care-free/careful (delete as appropriate) instruction of player/teacher David Moss.

1993 [Personal Prototype] - The Privatisation of Britains Rail Network

18xx - Intermediate - Sunday 09:00-14:00

Host: Dave Moss

Ideal player count: 5
Remaining player spots: 3
Get a first class ticket for trying Dave's naescent 18xx design. Hark! Look how he throws the 18xx naming convention out of the window! But what conventions remain?

Who are The Train Rush?

Your hosts, Craig Taylor and Dave Moss, are the co-presenters of The Train Rush, a short form podcast, typically running 30-45 minutes in length, where they discuss a single 'train game' they have played in the preceding fortnight.

Focusing on 18xx games but also covers related games, when sufficiently compelling.
